About hair day

Hair Day is a passionate and skilled hair salon specializing in cut, color, and styling. Led by Pria, a certified colorist with a flair for edgy coloring techniques, our team creates personalized looks that bring out the best in each client. With a warm and relaxing atmosphere, we offer a rejuvenating experience where clients can treat themselves to a hair makeover. We believe in self-expression through hair and take the time to understand our clients' vision. Book in advance to secure your spot and experience the magic of Hair Day. Beyond hair, we also offer makeup and nail services, completing your beauty routine. Trust us to make you feel confident and beautiful every day.
